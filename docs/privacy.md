# Privacy Policy for Clipcat

**Effective Date:** April 19, 2026

This privacy policy applies to the Clipcat Android app and Windows application.

## 1. Information Collection and Use
Clipcat is designed to be a secure, local-first clipboard sharing tool. We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal information to any third-party servers. 

## 2. Camera Permission
The Clipcat Android app requires the **Camera** permission (`android.permission.CAMERA`). 
* **Why it's needed:** This permission is used strictly to scan QR codes displayed on your Windows PC to securely pair your devices.
* **Data handling:** The camera stream is processed locally on your device in real-time. We do not record, save, or upload any images or video data.

## 3. Network and Clipboard Data
Clipcat transfers your clipboard data (text, images, files) directly between your paired devices over your local network using secure AES-GCM encryption. Your clipboard data never leaves your local network and is never sent to our servers or any third parties.

## 4. Third-Party Services
We do not use any third-party analytics or advertising services in Clipcat.

## 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. 

## 6. Contact Us
If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
